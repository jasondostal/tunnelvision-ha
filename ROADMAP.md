# Roadmap

## v0.2.0 — Switches & Diagnostics

- [ ] Switch entity for killswitch (on/off toggle instead of two buttons)
- [ ] Switch entity for VPN (connect/disconnect toggle)
- [ ] Diagnostics download (config, entity states, coordinator data)
